Hearty Viking Stew Recipe

How to Make Hearty Viking Stew

Hearty and rustic, Vikings relied on this traditional stew to fuel their bodies and souls on harsh winter nights. Bursting with the robust flavors, Viking Stew is traditionally simmered in a rich broth of beef, lamb, root vegetables, thyme, and juniper berries. This hearty meals perfectly captures the spirit of the Old Norse hearth--simple, nourishing, and sublime on cold nights by the fire.
